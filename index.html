Jedi

Jedi are the knights that keep galaxy's in order. They believe in selflessness and they fight the dark side and other bad people. They use the force which can move objects and they use lightsabers.

Dark Side

The dark side is the dark side of the force where users rely on hatred, anger, and fear to power their force unlike the jedi who rely on selflessness and helping others.. Users of the dark side are often corupted jedi who turn to hatred and use their powers to power that hatred.

Jedi Order

The Jedi Order, later known as the Old Jedi Order and referred to as the Holy Order of the Jedi Knights, was an ancient monastic peacekeeping organization unified by its belief and observance of the Force, specifically the light side.
